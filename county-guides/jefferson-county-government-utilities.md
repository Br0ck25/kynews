# Jefferson County, Kentucky -- Government Offices & Utilities Guide

*Last Updated: March 2026*

> **Note:** Jefferson County and the City of Louisville operate as a **consolidated metropolitan government** known as Louisville Metro Government. Traditional county offices such as a county judge/executive are replaced by a Metro Mayor and Metro Council. Services below reflect the Louisville Metro structure.
