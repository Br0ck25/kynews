## Primary County Offices

### County Judge/Executive

**Office Name:** Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government (LFUCG) -- Mayor's Office  
**Address:** 200 E. Main Street, Lexington, KY 40507  
**Phone:** (859) 258-3000  
**Website:** https://www.lexingtonky.gov

> **Note:** Fayette County operates under a consolidated city-county government known as the Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government (LFUCG). The Mayor serves as the chief executive. There is no separate County Judge/Executive.
